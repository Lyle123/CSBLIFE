10 new things that I learned from St. La Salle

1.The first son of a very wealthy parents living in France.

2.Receive the tonsure at age 11

3.Was named Canon at age 16 at at Reims Cathedral family affairs after his parents died

4.Ordained priest April 9, 1678

5.Beatified February 19, 1888

6.Died April 7, 1719 at Saint Yon near Rouen

7.Was a pioneer in founding training colleges for teachers

8.In 1900 he was declared as a Saint.

9.Proclaimed Patron of Christian Teachers May 15, 1950

10.Full name: Jean-Baptiste de La Salle
